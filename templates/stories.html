Little Jerry

This little lovely fellow has been rescued by us outisde Cringe City, in Cringesty. He was inside of a blue bag, crying out loud for someone to come to their rescue...Luckily, our lovely staff member Maria Cringenovicky happened to pass by him. Ever since, against the vicissitudes of fate, this big guy has been an important part of our little community! You can imagine that his safety and well-being will without a doubt remain a great cause of concern for us, even after his being taken into care by someone else.
